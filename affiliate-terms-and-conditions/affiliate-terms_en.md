## 3. Commission Structure and Incentive Mechanism

### 3.1 Basic Commission
- When new users who registered through your Exclusive Affiliate Link successfully purchase paid membership services for the first time, you can receive 40% of the actual payment amount for that order as commission (i.e., "First Payment Commission");
- For subsequent renewal orders from that user, you can receive 36% of the actual payment amount for the order as commission (i.e., "Renewal Commission");
- All commissions are calculated based on users' actual payment amounts. That is, when users use promotional codes, coupons, or receive price reductions through discount activities, commissions are calculated based on the actual payment amount after reduction. For example, if a new user uses a 20% discount to purchase a package originally priced at $100, their actual payment amount is $80, and the Affiliate Member receives $32 as First Payment Commission ($80 Ã— 40%).

### 3.2 Monthly Performance Incentives
To motivate excellent Affiliate Members, we have established a Monthly Performance Incentive mechanism. Based on the number of Valid Orders successfully promoted in the current month, eligible Affiliate Members can enjoy corresponding additional commission rewards in the following month.

#### 3.2.1 Statistical Period
The statistical period for Monthly Performance Incentives is from 00:00 to 23:59 on the last day of the month in Singapore Standard Time, with statistics based on order generation time.

#### 3.2.2 Incentive Implementation Rules

| Current Month Valid Orders | Next Month Additional Commission |
|---------------------------|--------------------------------|
| 50-99 orders | Additional +2.5% |
| 100-149 orders | Additional +5% | 
| 150-199 orders | Additional +7.5% | 
| 200 orders and above | Additional +10% | 

**Example**: If you successfully promote 100 Valid Orders in January, all confirmed commissions in February will receive an additional 5% on top of the basic commission calculation, for example:
- First Payment Commission becomes 45% of users' actual payment amount (basic 40% + incentive 5%);
- Renewal Commission becomes 41% of users' actual payment amount (basic 36% + incentive 5%).

**Important Note**: Performance incentives are calculated based on all confirmed commissions in the following month and cannot be accumulated or transferred across months.

### 3.3 Tracking and Attribution Mechanism
